Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a visionary. A visionary is an individual that wishes to explore and innovate upon the world to make it better. Someone who wishes to make advancements and dedicate their lives towards achieving their dream.

I chose Elon Musk for many reasons such as:

  • Dedication and Work Ethic- Elon is willing to put actions behind his words working hundreds of hours a week.
  • Commitment- When Tesla and SpaceX were both near bankruptcy in 2008 Elon invested all of his remaining funds into both companies. Article and Interview
  • Ambition- Elon Musk has ambition looking to innovate the world in ways thought unimaginable.
  • Vision- Elon has a grand vision of what tomorrow could become and is willing to invest- putting his money where his mouth is.

Elon Musk's focus is on the future and while recent news has been focused on his wealth. He is almost single-handedly responsible for a plethora of innovation including the push towards the mass adoption of electric automobiles.
